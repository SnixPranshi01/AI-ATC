Best Bovine Breed

Gir

Heat-resistant and high-yielding, Gir cows from Gujarat are known for their rich, nutritious milk

Sahiwal

Punjab's finest milking breed, Sahiwal cows excel in heat tolerance, disease resistance, and superior milk quality.

Hallikar

Indigenous draught breed from Karnataka. Known for endurance, speed, and excellent draught power, though milk yield is low.

Kangayam

An indigenous draught breed from Tamil Nadu. Renowned for its endurance, speed, and excellent draught power, though milk yield is modest.

Jersey

A small to medium-sized dairy breed from the Channel Islands, typically light fawn to dark brown with a white face and dark eyes.

Red Sindhi

Hardy and adaptable, Red Sindhi cows from Sindh thrive in hot climates, producing high-fat milk.

Holstein Friesian

The Holstein Friesian is a globally recognized dairy breed, originating from the Netherlands and Germany. It is characterized by its distinctive black-and-white or red-and-white coat and is known for its high milk production.
